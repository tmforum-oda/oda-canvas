# TMFO004 Credentials Management Operators

Automates the secure provisioning and ongoing lifecycle management of credentials for ODA Components. It removes the risk and operational overhead of manually handling sensitive client secrets by ensuring credentials are created, rotated, and retired automatically — reducing the attack surface and keeping components continuously authenticated with the enterprise identity platform.

At present, there is one implementation:

* [credentials-management](./credentials-management/): Implementation of a Credentials Management Operator for Kubernetes.
